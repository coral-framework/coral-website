co.SystemStateenum

Enumeration of states in the system’s life cycle.

The two main system states are nil (uninitialized) and running (fully functional). All the other states are transient and only observable during the system’s setup and teardown.

  • Tags:
    • System
  • Referenced from 1 type: co.ISystem.

Identifiers

0SystemState_None

Pre-initialization state. Only the Coral module is available.

1SystemState_Initializing

Modules are loading. They cannot interact yet.

2SystemState_Integrating

Modules are initialized and can now interact with each other.

3SystemState_Integrated

Modules are integrated and functional (without presentation).

4SystemState_IntegratingPresentation

Modules are setting up their presentation.

5SystemState_Running

The system is running. The presentation is live.

6SystemState_Disintegrating

The system is being torn down.
